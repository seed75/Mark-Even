About Even & Mark

Even & Mark is a Melbourne-based professional conference organiser built for associations and member bodies. We manage the full life of a conference — venue to wrap-up — with the marketing handled by the same people who hold the run sheet.

The name is the offer: Even for the events we run, Mark for the marketing we build in. It's one team because your conference is one project, however many disciplines it takes.

We work across Australia & Aotearoa New Zealand, from annual scientific meetings to member summits and everything on the calendar in between.
